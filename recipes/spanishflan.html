Spanish Flan

Description:

Spanish flan is a baked custard, smooth dessert smothered with caramel sauce!

  • Prep Time: 15min
  • Cook Time: 1hr
  • Additional Time: 3hr
  • Total Time: 4hr 15min
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions:

  • Step 1:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  • Step 2:

    1. Melt sugar in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat until liquefied and golden in color.
    2. Carefully pour hot syrup into a deep 9-inch round glass baking dish, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom.
    3. Set aside.

  • Step 3:

    1. Beat eggs in a large bowl. Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla; beat until smooth.
    2. Pour egg mixture on top of caramel in the baking dish; place in a deep roasting pan.
    3. Carefully pour in enough hot water to come 1 inch up the sides of the dish.

  • Step 4:

    1. Bake in the preheated oven until just set with a slight jiggle in the center, about 1 hour.
    2. Keep in mind the flan will continue to set as it cools.
    3. Remove from the oven and carefully transfer baking dish to a wire rack.
    4. Cool to room temperature, then cover with plastic wrap to prevent a skin forming.
    5. Refrigerate for 3 hours or up to 3 days.

  • Step 5:

    1. To serve, run a knife around the edges of the dish.
    2. Carefully invert onto a rimmed serving plate and let caramel sauce flow over flan.
