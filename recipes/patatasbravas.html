Patatas Bravas

Description:

Patatas bravas are fried potatoes that are fiercely textured, fiercely flavored, fiercely presented, and fiercely enjoyed!

  • Prep Time: 25min
  • Cook Time: 20min
  • Additional Time: 45min
  • Total Time: 1hr 30min
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • Dipping Sauce:

    • 1 clove garlic, minced, or more to taste
    • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1 pinch salt
    • 1 cup mayonnaise
    • 1 tablespoon sherry vinegar, or more to taste
    • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
    • ¼ teaspoon ground chipotle peppers
    • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste

  • Spice Blend:

    • 1 tablespoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
    • ½ teaspoon paprika
    • ½ teaspoon ground chipotle peppers

  • Potatoes:

    • 2 quarts cold water
    • 1 tablespoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
    • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying

Directions:

  • Step 1: Make dipping sauce

    1. Combine garlic, smoked paprika, and salt in a mortar and pestle or food processor; process until smooth.
    2. Mix in mayonnaise, sherry vinegar, tomato paste, chipotle chile powder, and cayenne pepper until combined.
    3. Cover and refrigerate sauce.

  • Step 2: Make spice blend

    1. Mix together salt, black pepper, paprika, and chipotle chile powder in a small bowl.
    2. Set aside.

  • Step 3: Make potatoes

    1. Pour water into a large saucepan.
    2. Stir salt, smoked paprika, cumin, and bay leaves into water.
    3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and cover; simmer for 10 minutes.

  • Step 4: Boil potatoes

    1. Stir potatoes into seasoned water.
    2. Bring to a boil, and simmer until potatoes are tender but not fully cooked, 4 to 5 minutes.
    3. Drain potatoes and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely, about 45 minutes.

  • Step 5: Fry potatoes

    1. Heat vegetable oil in a large cast iron skillet or Dutch oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Add cooled potato cubes and cook, stirring frequently, until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes.
    3. Transfer potatoes to a baking sheet lined with paper towels to cool slightly.
    4. Toss in a bowl with spice blend and serve with dipping sauce.
